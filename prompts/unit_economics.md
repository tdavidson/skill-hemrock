# Task prompts — Unit Economics Tool

## Orientation

### Explain the unit economics calculation

Walk me through how this Unit Economics Tool calculates CAC, LTV, payback, and contribution margin. For each, explain the formula being used and which inputs drive it.

### Map your business inputs into the model

My business is [describe: e.g. 'B2B SaaS at $200/mo with 4% monthly churn, 80% gross margin, $5k blended CAC']. Help me map these into the model's inputs and confirm what the resulting LTV/CAC ratio and payback period would be.

## Analysis

### Compare LTV/CAC across scenarios

Show me LTV/CAC under three scenarios: pessimistic, base, and optimistic on [the variable that worries me most, e.g. churn or CAC]. Walk me through which input changes for each scenario and what the resulting LTV/CAC and payback look like.

### Find break-even CAC

Given my current LTV assumption, what is the maximum CAC I can spend and still have a healthy LTV/CAC ratio (say, 3x or above)? Walk me through the calculation.

### Sensitivity analysis on churn

Show me how LTV, payback, and LTV/CAC change as monthly churn moves across [range, e.g. 2% to 10%]. Explain why LTV is so sensitive to churn at the low end and less sensitive at the high end.

## Customization

### Add a contribution margin layer

My business has variable costs beyond COGS — [describe: e.g. payment processing, fulfillment, support]. Walk me through where to add these so the contribution margin used in the payback calculation is accurate.
