# Task prompts — Runway & Cash Budget

## Orientation

### Explain the runway model

Walk me through this Runway and Cash Budget model. What inputs do I need to provide, what does it calculate automatically, and what outputs should I focus on?

## Analysis

### Calculate runway

Based on the current inputs — cash balance, monthly burn, and any revenue — what is my projected runway in months? Walk me through the calculation so I understand what is driving it. Which single assumption, if changed, would have the biggest impact on runway?

### Find the break-even point

Based on the current expense and revenue inputs, when does the model project the business reaches cash flow break-even — where monthly inflows equal or exceed outflows? Which assumptions most influence that timing?

### Model expense cuts for extended runway

I want to model what happens to runway if I cut [describe cuts, e.g. reduce headcount by 2, eliminate a vendor contract]. Before making changes, show me which expense inputs are affected and by how much, then calculate the new runway.
